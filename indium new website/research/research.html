Our Research Area

Wearable Biosensors

At Indium, we specialize in developing advanced wearable biosensors that leverage innovative nanomaterials for real-time health monitoring. Our cutting-edge devices seamlessly integrate into daily life, providing personalized health insights. Through our research, we aim to transform diagnostics and treatment, empowering individuals with proactive health solutions.

Fuel Cells

Indium is committed to advancing fuel cell technology by harnessing the potential of innovative nanomaterials to improve performance, efficiency, and sustainability. Our research focuses on developing next-generation fuel cells that provide eco-friendly, high-performance energy solutions, contributing to a cleaner, more sustainable future for everyone.

Nanogenerators

Triboelectric nanogenerators (TENGs) are innovative devices that convert mechanical energy into electrical power, harnessing the power of motion. At Indium, we are developing advanced TENGs using cutting-edge nanomaterials to enhance energy harvesting efficiency. Our research aims to provide sustainable energy solutions, empowering self-powered devices and contributing to a greener future.

Nanogenerators

Photodetectors are essential devices that convert light into electrical signals, enabling advanced optical sensing and communication. At Indium, we are pushing the boundaries of photodetector technology by incorporating cutting-edge nanomaterials to enhance sensitivity, speed, and efficiency. Our research is focused on advancing optical technologies for applications in healthcare, energy, and more.
