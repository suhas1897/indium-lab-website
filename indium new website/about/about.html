About Us

Indium Laboratory

Welcome to Indium, where innovation meets impact. Established on February 28, 2024, under the aegis of the MURTI Research Center at GITAM University, Bengaluru Campus, Indium is the culmination of visionary leadership and unwavering institutional support through an internal funding scheme.


Our mission is to drive advancements in Innovative Nanomaterial Development for Integrated Utility in health monitoring and clean energy research. At Indium, we are at the forefront of creating cutting-edge solutions in:

  • Wearable Biosensors: Revolutionizing health monitoring with real-time, accurate diagnostics.
  • Fuel Cells: Innovating sustainable energy sources for a cleaner future.
  • Triboelectric Nanogenerators (TENGs): Harnessing mechanical energy for powering the next generation of devices.
  • Photodiodes: Enhancing optical technologies with advanced nanomaterials.
  • Our multidisciplinary approach combines science, technology, and sustainability to solve pressing global challenges. With the blessings of our leadership team and a collaborative research ethos, Indium is poised to make significant contributions to the fields of health monitoring and clean energy.

    • Vision

    Indium will be a global leader in nanomaterial innovations, driving excellence with integrity and compassion to transform health and energy solutions.

    Mission

  • Develop innovative nanomaterial-based technologies to revolutionize health monitoring and sustainable energy solutions.
  • Foster a culture of integrity, collaboration, and compassion in advancing cutting-edge research for societal impact.
  • Bridge science and industry by delivering practical, high-impact technologies in wearable biosensors, fuel cells, and energy harvesting systems.
